Mykhailo — Founder

I got into car shipping the hard way. Five years ago, I started at one of the largest auto transport brokerages in the United States and worked my way up from the ground. Hundreds of phone calls a day, learning the carrier market inside out, figuring out why customers get burned and how the system actually works.

Over the years, I've handled more than a thousand shipments across all 50 states. Private clients relocating cross-country, dealerships moving inventory from auctions, collectors shipping high-value vehicles in enclosed trailers. I've seen every scenario: tight deadlines, difficult routes, carrier no-shows, price disputes, chargebacks. I know what goes wrong and how to prevent it.

I started BladeHaul because I got tired of watching customers get lied to. The industry standard is to quote low, go silent, and then squeeze people for more money. I've seen it happen thousands of times from the inside. BladeHaul is built on doing the opposite: honest pricing, daily communication, and giving you real options instead of pressure.

Based in Ukraine, operating in the US market. Every shipment is managed personally. No call centers, no runaround, no scripts. Just real talk and real results.
